The Corriewood Group

Established in 1985, The Corriewood Group, is a family-owned healthcare leader providing nursing, residential and disability care in picturesque locations throughout Ireland.

Our vision of care is underpinned by our person-centred approach to ensure each home is a welcoming and vibrant place where independence, choice, dignity and the highest standards of care are valued above all else.

Our Services at Corriewood Group

It is our aim to offer a range of services across our homes, which enhance the quality of life our service users. Our homes provide personal and social care, practical support and other related domiciliary services. We recognise that this can only happen by employing staff who are trained to deliver a high-quality professional service to our clients.

  • Nursing Care
  • Residential Care
  • Domiciliary Care
